Cooper for Constable

Sheldon Cooper was born and raised in Galvaston, Texas. He has been interested in science since a young child and recently retired from the realm of academia. Sheldon moved to Lexington, Kentucky a few years ago with his wife Amy and their 2 young children.

Sheldon Cooper is running for Constable as he is looking to serve the people of Lexington, Kentucky and provide vital services to his constituents. He has done extensive amounts of research to determine effective ways to perform the duties of Constable.

Reasons to Vote Cooper for Constable

  • Sheldon will give back to the local economy by utilizing local ride-sharing companies to complete his duties.
  • Sheldon will ensure that all persons are treated equally.
  • Sheldon will support other local officials to ensure continuity across Lexington.
